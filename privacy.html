Your privacy is important. Here's how algebrain.dev handles your data.

Everything stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared.

What We Store

algebrain.dev stores only two things in your browser's local storage:

  • Your nickname (so you can track your progress)
  • Your game scores (to show your personal high scores)

This data stays in your browser only. It never leaves your device.

What We Don't Do

  • No tracking or analytics
  • No data collection of any kind
  • No uploads to servers
  • No telemetry
  • No commercial use
  • No monetization
  • No sharing with third parties
  • No cookies (except browser localStorage for your scores)

Your Nickname

When you start playing, you'll be asked for a nickname. This is just for displaying on your personal scoreboard. Use any name you like ‚Äì it's stored only in your browser.

Your Scores

When you complete games with perfect accuracy, your scores are saved in your browser's localStorage. These scores are visible only to you on your device. No one else can see them.

Our Mission

algebrain.dev exists for one simple purpose: to help kids learn math through fun games. There's no business model, no data harvesting, no ulterior motives. Just games for learning.
